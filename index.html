Philosophy

Considered introductions
where alignment already exists.

Reputation compounds. So does error.

We introduce selectively and deliberately. We do not broker transactions, circulate lists, or operate at volume. Every engagement begins with a private assessment of alignment and intent.

If we introduce, preparation has been completed and both parties understand consequence. We do not publish client names or publicise outcomes.

Trust is cumulative — and remains personal.

Scope

Narrow by design

Meaningful introductions where context, timing, and intent are already aligned. We do not facilitate speculative outreach or exploratory volume networking.

Established Organisations

Investors & Principals

Private Individuals
